Services

Our Services

Background Screening

We are fully qualified and equipped to conduct in-house background screening, suitability and probity checks, which are essential pre-hiring screening procedures in today's labour market that verify a candidate's work history, education, and criminal and credit records.

Biometric Scanning

We use biometric fingerprint scanners to authenticate and verify personal and confidential information. Using technologies like fingerprint or facial recognition, for enhanced security, access control, and time tracking, offering a more secure and efficient alternative.

Verification

Academic qualifications, personal characteristics and capabilities according to the vacancy profile. Confirming an applicant's identity, qualifications, and work history to ensure they are who they claim to be and possess the necessary skills and experience for the role.

Criminal Record Checks

We provide full criminal record checks including arrest records, with written consent from the candidate, ensuring the check is relevant to the job requirements, adhering to the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

Credit Checks

We provide thorough credit worthiness checks including character reference checks to gain insight into the candidate's financial responsibility, particularly if the prospective role involves handling the company's finances.

Driver's Licenses

We verify and confirm driver's licenses, forklift license and PDP licenses for authenticity, validity, and status of a candidate's license, ensuring compliance with driving laws and regulations, and mitigating potential risks.
