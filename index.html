About

Find Out More About Us

Apex Labour Solutions was founded in 2015

With over 10 years of extensive Supply Chain Management experience.

  • A proud member of NBCRFLI

    Apex Labour Solutions has been a compliant member of the National Bargaining Council for the Road Freight and Logistics Industry (NBCRFLI).

  • After several years servicing the Road Freight & Logistics Industry

    We believe that we have now gained the necessary experience & expertise to diversify into other industries i.e. The Metal, Rubber, Food & Industries that are governed by the Basic Condition of Employment Act (BCEA)

We are dedicated to rendering the highest standard of service to our clients and continuously aim to foster close relationships with them.
